Our Story

A Neighborhood Gem with
a Culinary Heart

Nestled within the Lakeland Village community along the serene shores of Hood Canal, The Bistro opened its doors in 2017 with one simple mission: to bring chef-driven cuisine and genuine hospitality to Allyn, Washington.

With sweeping views of our adjacent golf course and tranquil pond, The Bistro offers the perfect backdrop for an intimate dinner, a lively gathering, or your next fully-catered event. Our culinary-trained chef brings creativity and craft to every plate — from our legendary house-made clam chowder to hand-crafted pasta dishes.

Whether you're joining us for a casual weeknight dinner, Sunday brunch, or planning an event of 200, we treat every guest like family.
